2. **Innominate artery reimplantation** (2 yrs, 12 kg) — major vascular in a toddler
3. **Pulmonary valve replacement** (13 yrs) — cardiac surgery, bypass exposure
4. **Posterior spinal fusion 13+ levels** (26 yrs) — major blood loss, neuromonitoring
5. **Posterior bone decompression/duraplasty** (7 yrs) — posterior fossa neurosurgery
