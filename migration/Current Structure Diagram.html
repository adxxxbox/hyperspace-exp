Decision 1
Delegated is active content
Tasks and calendar stay in Workspace because they are about the User's life. May add more delegated content as needed.
Decision 2
Operator logs live with operators
Each operator folder contains its .OPERATOR.md protocol and matching .LOG.md history file, not the managed task/calendar content itself.
Principle 1
Minimal context loading
Operators should load the smallest useful context: shared rules, their own protocol, and only the Workspace material needed for the current task.
Principle 2
Archive means inactive
_agentic/archive/ may preserve retired User material, but its presence there means it is no longer active Workspace material.
